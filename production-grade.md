✦ In a production-grade Kubernetes environment, we don't use port-forward. Instead, we use a combination of Ingress Controllers, Managed Certificates, and External DNS.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            

  Here is how a production HTTPS setup typically looks:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             

  1. The Traffic Flow (The "Chain")                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  In production, traffic follows this path:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
  User -> DNS (argocd.example.com) -> Cloud Load Balancer -> Ingress Controller (e.g., Nginx) -> Argo CD Service -> Argo CD Pod.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  2. Components of a Production Setup                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  A. Ingress Controller (The Gatekeeper)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  Instead of exposing the service directly, we use an Ingress Controller (like NGINX, Traefik, or Istio).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
   * It acts as a reverse proxy.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
   * It sits at the edge of your cluster and handles all incoming traffic.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  B. TLS Termination (The Encryption)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  In production, encryption is usually handled at the Ingress level (this is called TLS Termination).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
   * The Ingress Controller holds the SSL certificate.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
   * It decrypts the traffic and passes it to the internal app (Argo CD) over the internal network.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
   * Note: Since the internal network is "trusted," the app can run in insecure mode internally while the user still sees a secure https:// connection.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  C. Cert-Manager (The Librarian)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   
  To avoid manually managing SSL certificates, production clusters use cert-manager (https://cert-manager.io/).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
   * It automatically requests certificates from authorities like Let's Encrypt or Google Managed Certificates.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
   * It automatically renews the certificates before they expire.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   
   * It stores the certificates as Kubernetes Secrets that the Ingress Controller uses.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  D. ExternalDNS (The Connector)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  This tool automatically syncs your Kubernetes Ingress resources with your DNS provider (like Google Cloud DNS, AWS Route53, or Cloudflare).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
   * When you create an Ingress for argocd.example.com, it automatically creates the DNS record for you.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  ---                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               

  3. Example Production Configuration (YAML)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  In production, instead of port-forwarding, you would apply an Ingress Resource that looks like this:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
    1 apiVersion: networking.k8s.io/v1                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
    2 kind: Ingress                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
    3 metadata:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
    4   name: argocd-server-ingress                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
    5   namespace: argocd                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
    6   annotations:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
    7     cert-manager.io/cluster-issuer: letsencrypt-prod # Automatically get a cert                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
    8     kubernetes.io/ingress.class: nginx              # Use Nginx controller                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
    9     nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/ssl-passthrough: "false"                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
   10     nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/backend-protocol: "HTTP" # Talk to Argo in insecure mode                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
   11 spec:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
   12   tls:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
   13   - hosts:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
   14     - argocd.example.com         # Your real domain                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
   15     secretName: argocd-tls-cert   # Where cert-manager will save the cert                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
   16   rules:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
   17   - host: argocd.example.com                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
   18     http:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
   19       paths:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
   20       - path: /                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
   21         pathType: Prefix                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
   22         backend:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
   23           service:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
   24             name: argocd-server                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
   25             port:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
   26               number: 80
